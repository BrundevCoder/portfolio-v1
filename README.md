# My Portfolio 🛠️
Welcome to my portfolio! In this project, you can see more details about me, learn about the programming languages ​​I work with, see my projects, and more soon!

## Why did I create this?
I created my portfolio to share with anyone who I am and what I enjoy working on, as well as to serve as a hub for my projects!

## About the design:
Compared to V1, I wanted something modern and more eye-catching, so I opted to choose bold colors that blend well with the rest of the site.

Here are some more details about the design:
- What colors did I use?
  > I opted for strong and eye-catching colors such as: `#fff` (pure white) background, and for text and other details, colors like `#FF8800` and also `#C98230`.

- What new fonts did I use?
  > Since the idea was to have something eye-catching and modern, I preferred fonts like `Holtwood One SC` and `Hanalei Fill` because they are 'different', and I think they go well with the aesthetics of my portfolio!

## about the animations:
